<p><p>The zeolite catalysts market is primarily divided into two types: natural zeolite catalysts and synthetic zeolite catalysts. Natural zeolite catalysts are derived from naturally occurring minerals and are often used for applications like adsorbents and ion-exchange processes due to their unique porous structure. Synthetic zeolite catalysts, on the other hand, are artificially manufactured to provide enhanced properties, tailored for specific chemical reactions, making them widely used in petroleum refining and petrochemical processes. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial applications.</p></p>
