<p><p>The rotavator blade market features three primary types: L blades, C blades, and J blades. L blades are ideal for shallow tillage and soil aeration, promoting optimal seedbed preparation. C blades specialize in deeper tillage, effectively turning and mixing soil for better fertility. J blades, with their unique curvature, excel in both cutting and mixing, providing versatility for various soil conditions. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs, enhancing efficiency and crop performance in different farming practices.</p></p>
