<p><p>The Spiral Pasteurizers market primarily features two heating methods: Direct Steam Heating and Indirect Steam Heating. Direct Steam Heating involves the direct injection of steam into the product, rapidly increasing the temperature for effective pasteurization. In contrast, Indirect Steam Heating uses heat exchangers to transfer steam heat to the product without direct contact, preserving flavor and quality. Both methods are crucial in enhancing food safety and shelf life, catering to diverse processing needs across various industries.</p></p>
