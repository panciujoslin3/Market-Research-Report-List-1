<p><p>The mining gas alarm market is categorized into two main types: stationary and portable gas alarms. Stationary gas alarms are permanently installed in mines to continuously monitor air quality and detect harmful gases, providing crucial safety alerts for workers. In contrast, portable gas alarms are handheld devices that can be easily transported, allowing workers to monitor air quality in various locations within the mining site. Both types play a vital role in enhancing safety and ensuring compliance with health regulations in the mining industry.</p></p>
