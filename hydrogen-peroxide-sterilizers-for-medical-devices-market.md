<p><ul><li>Vaporized Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilizers</li><li>Fogging Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen peroxide sterilizers market for medical devices includes primarily two types: vaporized hydrogen peroxide (VHP) sterilizers and fogging hydrogen peroxide sterilizers. VHP sterilizers utilize vaporized hydrogen peroxide to sterilize heat-sensitive instruments in enclosed chambers, ensuring effective microbial reduction. In contrast, fogging hydrogen peroxide sterilizers disperse a fine mist of hydrogen peroxide solution to sanitize surfaces and equipment, often used for larger areas. Both methods are efficient, environmentally friendly, and safe alternatives for maintaining sterile environments in healthcare settings.</p></p>
