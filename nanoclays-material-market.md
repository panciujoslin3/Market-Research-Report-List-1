<p><p>The nanoclays material market is primarily categorized into three types: Kaolinite, Smectite, and Other. Kaolinite, known for its excellent thermal stability and high chemical resistance, is widely used in packaging and ceramics. Smectite, including montmorillonite, is prized for its swelling properties, making it ideal for adhesives and coatings. The "Other" category encompasses various lesser-known clays that offer unique properties for specialized industrial applications, such as environmental remediation and pharmaceuticals, reflecting the diverse uses of nanoclays across multiple sectors.</p></p>
