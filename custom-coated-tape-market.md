<p><strong>The Custom Coated Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Coated Tape</li><li>Single Coated Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom coated tape market consists primarily of two types: double coated tape and single coated tape. Double coated tape features adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for bonding two surfaces together, providing strong adhesion and versatility in applications like laminating and mounting. In contrast, single coated tape has adhesive on just one side, commonly used for purposes like sealing, splicing, or packaging. Both types are valued in various industries for their specific adhesion properties and functional applications.</p></p>
