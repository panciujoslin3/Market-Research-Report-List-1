<p><strong>The Adjustable Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Foldable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adjustable Intraocular Lens market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid and foldable lenses. Rigid lenses, made from materials like polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA), offer stability and precision but require larger incisions for implantation. Foldable lenses, often crafted from silicone or acrylic, can be folded, allowing for smaller incisions and minimally invasive procedures. This flexibility in implantation techniques enhances patient comfort and recovery, driving the increasing preference for foldable lenses in contemporary cataract surgery.</p></p>
