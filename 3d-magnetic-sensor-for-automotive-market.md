<p><strong>The 3D Magnetic Sensor for Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors</li><li>Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive market for 3D magnetic sensors is primarily divided into two types: linear 3D magnetic sensors and rotary 3D magnetic sensors. Linear 3D magnetic sensors measure the magnetic field along three axes, detecting linear movements and positions, often used in applications like throttle position sensing. In contrast, rotary 3D magnetic sensors measure rotational movement around three axes, commonly utilized in steering angle and wheel speed sensors. Both types enhance vehicle performance and safety by providing precise spatial data.</p></p>
