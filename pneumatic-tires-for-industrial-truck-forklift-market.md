<p><p>The pneumatic tires market for industrial trucks, particularly forklifts, offers two main types: solid pneumatic tires and traditional pneumatic tires. Solid pneumatic tires are made entirely of solid rubber, providing excellent durability and resistance to punctures, making them ideal for rough surfaces and heavy loads. In contrast, traditional pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering better shock absorption and traction on smooth surfaces but are more vulnerable to punctures. Each type caters to different operational needs in industrial settings.</p></p>
