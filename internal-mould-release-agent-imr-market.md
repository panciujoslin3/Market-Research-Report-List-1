<p><p>Internal Mould Release Agents (IMR) are crucial in manufacturing processes to facilitate the easy removal of components from molds. The market for IMRs primarily consists of two types: solvent-based IMR and water-based IMR. Solvent-based IMRs offer excellent release properties and are often preferred for high-performance applications, though they may pose environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Conversely, water-based IMRs are more environmentally friendly, providing adequate release capabilities with lower environmental impact, making them increasingly popular in various industries.</p></p>
