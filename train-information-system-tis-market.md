<p><p>The Train Information System (TIS) is a digital infrastructure designed to manage and disseminate data related to train operations, enhancing passenger experience and safety. Market types for TIS include:</p><p>1. **Ring Type**: Features interconnected nodes in a circular layout, ensuring data transmission remains intact even if a segment fails.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Tree Type**: Organizes nodes hierarchically, promoting efficient data flow from central to peripheral systems.</p><p>3. **Star Topology Type**: Central hub connects various nodes, allowing for easy isolation and management of network traffic. Each type optimizes data communication and system resilience in rail transport.</p></p>
