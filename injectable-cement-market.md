<p><p>The injectable cement market is categorized into three main types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are typically used for applications requiring easy flow and filling of small spaces, making them ideal for minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity cements offer a balance of flow and stability, suitable for various orthopedic and dental applications. High viscosity cements, on the other hand, provide superior structural support and are often used in load-bearing applications where durability is essential.</p></p>
