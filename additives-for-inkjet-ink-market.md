<p><p>The inkjet ink additives market encompasses various types that enhance ink performance. Dispersing agents ensure uniform pigment distribution, preventing clumping. Leveling agents promote smooth ink flow, enhancing surface finish. Defoamers eliminate air bubbles, improving print quality. Emulsifiers stabilize mixtures of oils and water, ensuring consistency. Corrosion inhibitors protect ink components from metal degradation. Other additives may include surfactants and biocides, further improving ink properties, enhancing stability, and extending shelf life, ultimately optimizing the printing process.</p></p>
