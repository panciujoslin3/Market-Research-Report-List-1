<p><p>The card reader-writer market comprises several types catering to various identification needs. Contact card reader-writers require physical contact with the card for data exchange, making them secure for transactions. Contactless card reader-writers utilize RFID technology, allowing swift transactions without direct contact, enhancing convenience. Biometric card reader-writers incorporate fingerprint or facial recognition for added security, often used in high-security settings. Magnetic-stripe card reader-writers read data from magnetic strips, commonly seen in credit and debit cards, offering a straightforward solution for payment processing.</p></p>
