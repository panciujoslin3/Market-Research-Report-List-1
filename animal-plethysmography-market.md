<p><p>Animal plethysmography is a vital technique used to analyze physiological parameters in animals, focusing primarily on two key market types: body plethysmography and respiratory organ plethysmography. Body plethysmography measures overall physical changes, assessing aspects like body mass and volume in various species. In contrast, respiratory organ plethysmography specifically examines respiratory functions, analyzing airflow and lung capacity. Both methodologies are essential for research in veterinary medicine, pharmacology, and veterinary diagnostics, providing critical insights into animal health and respiratory conditions.</p></p>
