<p><p>The sodium tripolyphosphate market is divided into two primary types: industrial grade and food grade. Industrial grade sodium tripolyphosphate is primarily used in manufacturing detergents, ceramics, and water treatment applications due to its effectiveness as a chelating agent and synergist. In contrast, food grade sodium tripolyphosphate is utilized as a food additive, serving functions such as a preservative and texturizer, particularly in processed meats and seafood, enhancing their moisture retention and overall quality. Both grades cater to distinct sectors with specific quality requirements.</p></p>
