<p><p>Fire Retardant Treated Plywood (FRTP) market features two main types: UCFA and UCFB. UCFA (Under Controlled Fire Authority) is treated to enhance resistance against fire, making it suitable for commercial applications where strict regulations apply. UCFB (Under Controlled Fire Barrier) offers heightened protection, often used in high-risk environments like industrial or high-rise construction. Both types are engineered to meet specific safety and performance standards, providing essential solutions for fire safety in various building projects.</p></p>
