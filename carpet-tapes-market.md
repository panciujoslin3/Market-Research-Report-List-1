<p><p>The carpet tapes market comprises two main types: tackified acrylic adhesive and natural rubber adhesive. Tackified acrylic adhesive offers strong bonding properties with excellent moisture resistance, making it suitable for various surfaces. It is favored for its durability and versatility in both indoor and outdoor applications. On the other hand, natural rubber adhesive is known for its high initial tack and flexibility, allowing for easy repositioning of carpets. This type is often preferred for residential use due to its lightweight, eco-friendly characteristics.</p></p>
