<p><p>The steel tire mold market comprises primarily two types: segmented molds and two-piece molds. Segmented molds are composed of several interchangeable sections, allowing for flexibility in tire design and ease of maintenance, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, two-piece molds consist of a single upper and lower part, offering efficiency in production and reduced complexity. Both types cater to the diverse needs of tire manufacturers, facilitating cost-effective and high-quality tire production.</p></p>
