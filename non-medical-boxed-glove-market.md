<p><p>The non-medical boxed glove market consists of three primary types: nitrile gloves, latex gloves, and vinyl gloves. Nitrile gloves are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for numerous applications. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort, often preferred for tasks requiring fine dexterity, although some may have allergy concerns. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and provide a looser fit, typically used for low-risk tasks and food handling. Each type caters to specific user needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
