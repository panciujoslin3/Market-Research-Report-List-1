<p><p>NFC systems encompass three primary market types: NFC readers, NFC chips, and NFC tags. NFC readers are devices that facilitate communication with NFC-enabled devices, enabling transactions and data exchange. NFC chips are embedded in various electronics and devices, allowing them to send and receive information wirelessly. NFC tags are passive components that store information, enabling quick access to data when scanned. Collectively, these components drive applications in mobile payments, access control, logistics, and marketing, enhancing connectivity and user interaction.</p></p>
