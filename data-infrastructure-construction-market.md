<p><p>The data infrastructure construction market encompasses various cell types that enhance network coverage and capacity. Macro cells are large installations providing extensive coverage in urban and rural areas. Micro cells serve smaller areas, improving capacity and quality in dense urban regions. Pico cells, even smaller, are used in specific locations like buildings for localized coverage. Femtocells are compact, low-power devices intended for home or small office use, ensuring robust connectivity in limited spaces, thereby enhancing the overall network performance.</p></p>
