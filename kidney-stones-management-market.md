<p><p>The kidney stones management market encompasses various treatment modalities, including Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL), ureteroscopy, and percutaneous nephrolithotomy. ESWL uses shock waves to break stones into smaller fragments, making them easier to pass. Ureteroscopy involves inserting a scope to remove or fragment stones directly from the urinary tract. Percutaneous nephrolithotomy is a more invasive procedure for large stones, involving a small incision in the back to extract them. These techniques cater to different stone types and patient needs.</p></p>
