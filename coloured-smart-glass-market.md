<p><strong>The Coloured Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coloured smart glass market encompasses three primary types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic smart glass changes tint and color in response to an electrical current, allowing for control over light and heat. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) smart glass utilizes suspended particles to adjust its opacity rapidly, offering on-demand privacy and light management. PDLC (Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystal) smart glass transitions from opaque to transparent when voltage is applied, effectively managing visibility and energy efficiency.</p></p>
