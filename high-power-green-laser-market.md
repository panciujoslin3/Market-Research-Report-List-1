<p><p>The high power green laser market encompasses two primary types: Quasi-Continuous Wave (QCW) lasers and Continuous Wave (CW) lasers. QCW lasers operate in short bursts, delivering high peak power while conserving energy, making them ideal for applications like precision marking and material processing. In contrast, CW lasers provide a constant output, suitable for applications requiring continuous energy, such as lighting and medical treatments. Each type serves distinct industries, reflecting varying demands for performance and application versatility.</p></p>
