<p><p>The PVDF membrane market is divided into two main types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic membranes. Hydrophobic PVDF membranes repel water, making them ideal for applications like filtration of gases and separation processes, where liquid management is crucial. In contrast, hydrophilic PVDF membranes attract water, enhancing their use in liquid filtration applications, such as in water treatment and biomedical fields. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on their interaction with liquids, driving innovation and demand in various sectors.</p></p>
